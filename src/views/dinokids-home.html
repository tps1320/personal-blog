Children have always been fascinated by dinosaurs, the massive reptiles that thundered across the Earth 200 million years ago. This blog is for all the kids who love dinosaurs. Taking my own son as an inspiration who loves and adores dinosaurs, I have created this blog. My son's interest in dinosaur started when he was about 3 years old. He is now 6 years old, and his love for dinosaurs has become more intense. He loves to draw them, watch videos about them and read books about dinosaurs:) His favourite dinosaur is Achilobator.
